3) You are allowed to put own screenshots and skyboxes of the game with visuals You are uploading (or screenshots of any skybox) in "Additional Information" field, but only from trusted resources (such as Gamebanana, Imgur, Pastebin, Discord, Google, Flickr etc). If not, this will result in denial.
3.1) You are allowed to request a removal of the content which has been uploaded to the site. This requires messaging from the telegram account which was included in information fields before. f.a.q

Q: I want to upload my visuals, how do I do it?
A: You can submit your own config at main page, by clicking "Upload Visuals".
Q: Someone just uploaded my own visuals without me knowing, what can I do?
A: Unfortunately, NVV cannot help. Once config is uploaded on the site, it has a right to stay under uploader's name. Unless the person who uploaded it will request a removal of content (He must follow the requirements in 3.1 of "Uploading a visual config").
Q: I submitted my visual config / mod and it still didn't appear on the site. Why?
A: To submit and verify / upload your visual config it can take from 1 to 6 days. If it did not appear even after, it was denied.
Q: What contacts I can leave when I am submitting my config?
A: As TOS says - You can leave both of your steam and telegram accounts. We suggest you to link steam account to be sure no one will claim your telegram username after you've changed it. But we also won't be able to respond to your request on removal of content since that requires your telegram account.
Q: I want to know where or at what state my visual config is. Can I know such information?
A: Uploader has a right to know where his visual config at. Such question can be answered at public telegram chat.
Q: I wanna help with site development! Is there any options?
A: Of course. Your suggestions will be listened at our public telegram chat or at GitHub.
Q: Why this site exists when I can make my own visuals and my friends has a lot of their own?
A: NVV understands that there are people who has been using Null Core for a long time. But there are also new people to that software which usually get lost when it comes to making their own visual config. It's also helpful if your account was reset or when you just want to try out other visual configs or take some of visual configs to base your own visual config on it.
