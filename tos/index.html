Uploading a visual config
1.0) Visuals should not be similar to already existing ones on the website, with some exceptions, like default Null Core's menu colorscheme.
1.1) It's highly recommended to fill all the fields when submitting a visual config and provide as much information as possible about it. This affects the time spent reviewing the visual config.
1.2) Submitted visual configs through the uploading page has a higher priority than configs that has been sent directly to the dev / public telegram chat.
1.3) As LA/TOS of NCL says - "NCL has not reviewed all of the websites linked to its website and is not responsible for the contents of any such linked website. The inclusion of any link does not imply endorsement by NCL. Use of any such linked website is at the user's own risk."
1.4) If You (User) have skyboxes and You would like to add them with Your uploaded visual config, You must provide the skyboxes in "Additional Information" field by putting a link (You must follow the 3.0) or writing in public telegram chat after uploading.
1.5) By uploading Your visual config You agree with NVV that You will provide Your IP address. Your IP address is stored privately and is used only in case of suspension. This does not apply to all pages on the website.
1.6) NVV appreciates the honesty and decency of a user. Therefore, NVV do not welcome indecent names of visual configs or their description. This behavior can lead to suspending Your IP from using our services.

2.0) Any kind of attempt to bypass uploading system (Spamming, using scripts to automatically upload more than one visual config, 3rd party web-bots, crawlers) will be considered as a threat to the website and it's services, resulting in Your, or, a threat's IP permanently suspended from using our services. If You cannot access the website or You think You were denied/suspended from using our services was a mistake, You can always reach out NVV at Telegram.
2.1) This is not an advertising site. Unfairly trying to claim someone's visual configs will result in a denial and in some cases - suspension.
2.2) Telegram links that have been linked to groups or chats instead of user will be removed or uploaded visual config denied. Incorrect telegram links also could be restored by messaging here.
2.3) If You did not message about Your telegram username being changed within 7 days since the day of uploaded visual config, it will be removed without any possible chance of restoring it.

3.0) You are allowed to put own screenshots and skyboxes of the game with visuals You are uploading (or screenshots of any skybox) in "Additional Information" field, but only from trusted resources (such as Gamebanana, Imgur, Pastebin, Discord, Google, Flickr etc). If not, this will result in denial.
3.1) You are allowed to request a removal of the content which has been uploaded to the website. This requires messaging from the telegram account which was included in information fields before. Unfortunately NVV does not cooperate with steam accounts or delete visual configs which has no known author/uploader.

F.A.Q

Q: I want to upload my visuals, how do I do it?
A: You can submit your own config at main page, by clicking "Upload Visuals"
Q: My game crashes when I'm trying to load a visual config, is the file corrupted?
A: It's a common thing for Null Core cheat to happen especially when it's a visual config. Most of the times after restarting the game it loads normally, however, if it still doesn't work, you should report it to us so we could check if the file is corrupted or not
Q: Someone just uploaded my own visuals without me knowing, what can I do?
A: Unfortunately, NVV cannot help. Once config is uploaded on the website, it has a right to stay under uploader's name. Unless the user who uploaded it will request a removal of content (User must follow the requirements of 3.1 of "Uploading a visual config")
Q: I submitted my visual config / mod and it still didn't appear on the website. Why?
A: To submit and verify / upload your visual config it can take from 1 to 6 days. If it did not appear even after, You can consider it was denied. Or you can always message us here
Q: What contacts I can leave when I am submitting my config?
A: As our TOS says - You can leave both of your steam and telegram accounts. We suggest you to link steam account to be sure no one will claim your telegram username after you've changed it. But we also won't be able to respond to your request on removal of content since that requires your telegram account
Q: I want to know where or at what state my visual config is. Can I know such information?
A: Yes, you can. Such question can be answered at public telegram chat
Q: I wanna help with project development! Is there any options?
A: Of course. Your suggestions will be listened at our public telegram chat or at GitHub
Q: Why this website exists when I can make my own visuals and my friends has a lot of their own?
A: NVV understands that there are people who has been using Null Core for a long time. But there are also new people to that software which usually get lost when it comes to making their own visual config. It's also helpful if your account was reset or when you just want to try out other visual configs or take some of visual configs to base your own visual config on it
