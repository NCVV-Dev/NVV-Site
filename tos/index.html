terms of service

Uploading a config
1) Visuals should not be similar to already existing ones on the site, with some exceptions, like default Null Core's menu colorscheme. 2) It's highly recommended to fill all the fields when submitting a visual config and provide as much information as possible about it. This increases the chances of the appearance of the config on the site and it's speed. 3) If user has skyboxes he would like to add with his uploaded visual config, he must provide the skyboxes in "Additional Information" field by putting a link or write to a public chat after uploading. 4) Any kind of attempt to bypass uploading system (spamming, using scripts to automatically upload many visual configs, etc.) will be considered as a threat to the site and it's services, resulting in your IP permanently suspended from using our services. 5) This is not an advertising site. Unfairly trying to claim someone's visuals will result in a denial and in some cases - suspension. 6) Telegram links that have been linked to groups or chats instead of user will be removed or denied. Incorrect telegram links also could be restored by messaging here. 7) If user did not message about his changed telegram username within 7 days, it will be removed without any possible chance of restoring it. 8) Submitted configs through the uploading page has a higher priority than configs that has been sent directly to dev / public chat. 9) As LA/TOS of NCL says - "NCL has not reviewed all of the websites linked to its website and is not responsible for the contents of any such linked website. The inclusion of any link does not imply endorsement by NCL. Use of any such linked website is at the user's own risk." 10) By uploading your visual config you agree with NVV that you will provide your IP address. Your IP address is stored privately and is used only in case of suspension. This does not apply to all pages on the site. 11) User is allowed to put own screenshots and skyboxes of the game with visuals he is uploading (or screenshots of any skybox) in "Additional Information" field, but only from trusted resources (such as Gamebanana, Imgur, Pastebin, Discord, Google, Flickr etc). If not, this will result in denial. 12) User is allowed to request a removal of the content which has been uploaded to the site by him from adminstration. This requires messaging from the telegram account which was included in information fields before. Unfortunately we do not cooperate with steam accounts or delete visual configs which has no known author/uploader.
Suggesting a mod
1) User is allowed to put links only from trusted resources (Gamebanana, Google Drive, Yandex Drive, Discord), if not, request will be denied. User is also allowed to put two or more links in one field. This is acceptable by uploading system as long as it doesn't exceed 2000 characters. 2) If the following link is not a mod or doesn't lead to one, request will be denied. If user continues to perform such actions, this will be considered as uploading system abuse and will result in suspension. 3) If current mod already exists on the page / user wants to upload similar one with some changes, user must include information about the mod in the "Include some information" field about the changes. Otherwise request will be denied. 4) By suggesting a mod you agree with NVV that you will provide your IP address. Your IP address is stored privately and is used only in case of suspension. This does not apply to all pages on the site.

f.a.q

Q: I want to upload my visuals, how do I do it?
A: You can submit your own config on this page.
Q: Someone just upload my own visuals without me knowing, what can I do?
A: Unfortunately, NVV cannot help. Once config is uploaded on the site, it has a right to stay under uploader's name.
Q: I submitted my config and it still didn't appear on the site. Why?
A: To submit and verify / upload your visual config it can take from 1 to 6 days. Such times can be explained by the fact that also with your uploaded config the site patch comes out.
Q: What contacts I can leave when I am submitting my config?
A: As uploading page says - You can leave both of your steam and telegram accounts. We suggest you to link steam account to be sure no one will claim your telegram username after you've changed it.
Q: I want to know where or at what state my visual config is. Can I know such information?
A: Uploader has a right to know where his visual config at. Such question can be answered at public telegram chat.
Q: I wanna help with site development! Is there any options?
A: Of course. Your suggestions will be listened at our public telegram chat or at GitHub.
Q: Can I suggest my own mod pack on the mods page?
A: Yes, user is allowed to provide pack of TF2 mods. NVV will review it and upload it under uploader's name shortly after. User can also choose to separate all of the mods from the mod pack so NVV will upload them all separately.
Q: How do I verify my mod I suggested on the mods page?
A: NVV reviews every mod equally and gives it a special rating which can value from 1 to 5. Every rated mod that goes after 2 to 3 is mod that might contain visual glitches or minor bugs. Ratings lower that 2 might contain potential glitches that can hurt your gameplay experience and ratings higher than 4 gets 'Mod is verified' with check mark in the shield icon.
Q: What does the colors and effects of the uploader's name represent?
A: This represents how much a person contributed to the site. Here's list of colors - - For contributing to the site in any kind - Greenish color - 5 uploaded mods/configs - Light blue color - 10 uploaded mods/configs - Light green color - 15 uploaded mods/configs - Purple color - 25 uploaded mods/configs - Magneta color - 35 uploaded mods/configs - Special effect - 45 uploaded mods/configs - Special effect - 50 uploaded mods/configs - Special color with badge - 60 uploaded mods/configs - Special effect - 70 uploaded mods/configs - Special effect - 80 uploaded mods/configs - Special effect - 100 uploaded mods/configs - Special effect with badge
