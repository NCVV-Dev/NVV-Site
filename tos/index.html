F.A.Q

Q: I want to upload my visuals, how do I do it?
A: You can submit your own config at main page, by clicking "Submit Visual Config"
Q: My game crashes when I'm trying to import a downloaded visual config, is the file corrupted?
A: Game crashes during the import of Null Core visual configs are a common occurrence. In most cases, restarting the game resolves the issue, and the configuration imports successfully. However, if your game continues to crash after multiple attempts, please report the issue along with the file details to our GitHub or Telegram. We will investigate whether the file is genuinely corrupted or if there is another issue
Q: Someone just submitted my own visuals I made without me knowing, what can I do?
A: Unfortunately, NVV cannot help with such scenario case. Once config is uploaded on the website, it has a right to stay under uploaders name.
Q: I submitted my visual config / mod and it still didn't appear on the website. Why?
A: Reviewing and verifying a visual configuration can take anywhere from 1 to 6 business days. If your submission does not appear after this period, it may have been denied. For further clarification, you can contact us via Telegram or other support channels to inquire about the status of your submission.
Q: What contacts I can leave when I am submitting my config?
A: As stated in our TOS, you may only provide your Steam account URL. If you submit a visual config using a different Steam account than the one used for previous submissions, the associated visual config will be updated to the latest Steam URL you have provided
Q: I want to know where or at what state my visual config is. Can I?
A: Yes, you can. There's no real track system behind so such question can be answered at public telegram chat
Q: I wanna help with the project development and I have cool ideas I can share! Is there any options?
A: Of course. Your suggestions will be listened at our public telegram chat or at GitHub. Your feedback and contributions are highly valued!
Q: Why this website exists when I can make my own visuals and my friends have a lot of their own?
A: NVV acknowledges that experienced users of Null Core often create their own visuals or share them with friends. However, this website is designed to assist newcomers who may find creating visual configs challenging or confusing. It also serves as a resource for those whose accounts have been reset, who wish to explore other visual configs, or who want to use existing configs as a foundation for creating their own
